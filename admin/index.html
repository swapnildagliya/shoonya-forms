Discount Rules
🎓
Student / Multi-class: 12% off — for students under 25 or enrolled in 2+ weekly classes. Price rounded UP to nearest euro (Math.ceil).
🎫
UiTPAS (kansentarief): 80% off — pay 20% of full price. Must be EXACT, never rounded. Formula: full_price × 0.2
📐
Prorated pricing (late joiners): full_price × (remaining / total sessions), rounded UP to nearest euro.
🆓
Free trial week: first week of each semester. Students can attend free — no registration required, just show up if class isn't full.
📝
Registration closes 3 weeks after the semester begins.
